Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 13, 2024

COPPA Compliant

For Parents

Salah Buddy is designed for children ages 7-10 to track their daily prayers during Ramadan. We take your child's privacy seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

1. Information We Collect

Information Provided by Parents

  • Parent Email Address: Used for account management, password recovery, and parental consent verification
  • Child's First Name: Displayed within the app for personalization

Information Generated by Use

  • Prayer Tracking Data: Which prayers your child marks as complete each day
  • Progress Statistics: Stars earned, streaks, and achievement milestones
  • Story Progress: Which daily stories have been watched

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Photos or videos of your child
  • Location data (we only use device location for prayer times, not stored)
  • Social media information
  • Contact lists or messages
  • Any other personal information

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Display your child's prayer progress within the app
  • Calculate streaks and award achievement stars
  • Show your child on the family leaderboard (if enabled)
  • Send password reset emails to parents when requested

3. Parental Consent

Before a child can use Salah Buddy with data storage features, we require:

  • A parent or guardian to create the account using their email address
  • Verification of parental consent via email confirmation
  • Parents maintain full control over their child's account

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell any personal information
  • Share data with third-party advertisers
  • Allow third parties to collect information through our app

We use Firebase (Google) for secure data storage and authentication. Firebase processes data according to their privacy policy.

5. Data Security

We protect your child's information by:

  • Using industry-standard encryption for data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Storing data securely in Firebase with strict access rules
  • Never storing passwords in plain text
  • Limiting data access to only what's necessary for app functionality

6. Your Rights

As a parent, you have the right to:

  • Review: See all data collected about your child
  • Delete: Request complete deletion of your child's data
  • Refuse: Decline further data collection (may limit app features)
  • Withdraw Consent: Remove consent at any time

How to Delete Your Child's Data

To delete all data associated with your child's account:

  1. Log in to the app with your parent account
  2. Go to Settings → Privacy → Delete Account
  3. Confirm the deletion request

Or email us at privacy@salahbuddy.app with your request.

7. Data Retention

We retain your child's data only for as long as the account is active. When you delete the account:

  • All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized usage statistics may be retained for app improvement

8. Guest Mode

Salah Buddy offers a "Guest Mode" that allows children to use the app without creating an account. In Guest Mode:

  • No personal information is collected
  • Progress is stored only on the local device
  • Data is not synced to our servers

9. Changes to This Policy

We will notify parents via email before making any material changes to this privacy policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us
