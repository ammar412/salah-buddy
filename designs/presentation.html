✨ The Vision

Make prayer feel like play. Not gamification—that's cheap. Make the act of tracking prayer itself delightful. The device should celebrate with the child.
— Steve Jobs perspective

🎯 The ONE Thing

Focus on prayer tracking done insanely well. Kids should feel like heroes every time they complete a prayer. Everything else is secondary.

Key Insight

Ship with ONE feature done perfectly: Prayer tracking that makes kids WANT to pray.

💡 Design Principles

  • Clarity over competition
  • One primary action per screen
  • Tactile feedback without noise
  • Grace, not guilt for missed prayers
  • Emotional design over metrics

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Who We're Designing For

🧒 Ages 4-7: Little Stars

  • Extra-bold, rounded typography
  • Large tap targets (44px minimum)
  • Single-step actions only
  • Parent co-use expected
  • Lots of animation & celebration
  • No reading required—icons only
👆 Big buttons 🎨 Bright colors 🎵 Sound effects

🧑 Ages 8-12: Rising Champions

  • Medium-weight geometric sans
  • More independence
  • Optional depth & challenges
  • Can navigate & read on their own
  • Light competition elements
  • Stats for those who seek them
📊 Progress stats 🏆 Challenges 👥 Friends
Don Norman's Advice

Don't split the interface—layer complexity. Same core UI for all, with progressive disclosure for advanced features.

🎮 Gamification That Feels Spiritual

🔓 Unlock, Don't Just Reward

  • Build your own mosque piece by piece
  • Collect prophets' stories
  • Earn charity coins to help virtual animals
  • Parent prayer together = multiplier
  • Random "mercy drops" keep it fresh

🔥 Streak Philosophy

  • Grace, not guilt: Dim the star, don't break the chain
  • Comeback mechanic: Make-up prayer window
  • Permanent wins: 7-day streak = keep one star forever
  • Recovery celebrated: "Most improved" badges

🏅 Badge Milestones

Spiritual, not arbitrary:

🌅 First Fajr ⭐ All 5 in One Day 🤝 Helped a Friend 🌙 Laylat al-Qadr

📚 Companions Collection

🏆 Inclusive Competition

⚖️ Key Design Decisions

✅ What We're Including

  • Prayer tracking as the hero feature
  • Companion character that grows with child
  • Personal constellation of completed prayers
  • Grace-based streaks (dim, don't break)
  • Multiple leaderboards (age, improvement, family)
  • Spiritual badges tied to meaningful moments
  • 30-day story arc with Eid finale
  • Parent dashboard (separate view)

❌ What We're Cutting/Deferring

  • Complex fasting tracker → v2 for older kids
  • Dashboard analytics → 3 metrics max
  • Global ranking list → Landscape instead
  • Notification spam → App waits for child
  • Points/currency → Unlock, don't score
  • Punitive visuals → Grace over guilt
  • Reading-dependent UI → Icons for young kids
The interface should disappear—focus on the ritual, not the app. Make 100 kids absolutely love it before trying to reach 10,000.
— Design Board Synthesis
