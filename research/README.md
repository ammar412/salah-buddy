## 📚 Research Files

### 1. [Islamic Design Research (HTML)](./islamic-design-research.html)
**Open in browser for best viewing**

The main research document covering:
- Simple Islamic geometric patterns suitable for children (6-pointed stars, 8-pointed stars, hexagons, arabesque)
- Traditional Ramadan and Islamic spirituality colors (greens, golds, purples, blues)
- Islamic design principles: Unity (Tawhid), Infinity, Symmetry
- Visual elements for prayer times (Fajr dawn, Dhuhr noon, Asr afternoon, Maghrib sunset, Isha night)
- Strategies to make Islamic design warm and child-friendly

**Key Takeaways:**
- 8-pointed star as primary pattern (ubiquitous in Islamic art, simple, symmetrical)
- Hexagon grid for prayer calendar (familiar honeycomb pattern)
- Prayer-specific colors (orange dawn for Fajr, blue for Dhuhr, purple night for Isha)
- Subtle background patterns at 5-10% opacity (adds texture without distraction)
- Blend geometric precision with warmth (rounded corners, soft gradients, gentle animations)

## 🌟 Key Design Decisions

### Primary Pattern: 8-Pointed Star
- **Why:** Ubiquitous in Islamic tilework, simple construction (two overlapping squares), instantly recognizable
- **Use:** Prayer time icons, achievement badges, decorative elements
- **Child-Friendly Adaptation:** Slightly rounded corners (2-4px border-radius), prayer-specific colors

### Prayer-Time Color System
| Prayer   | Time        | Color                  | Symbolism                |
|----------|-------------|------------------------|--------------------------|
| Fajr     | Dawn        | Orange (#FF6F00)       | Sunrise, fresh start     |
| Dhuhr    | Noon        | Sky Blue (#1E88E5)     | Bright daylight, clarity |
| Asr      | Afternoon   | Gold (#F5B526)         | Transition, golden hour  |
| Maghrib  | Sunset      | Deep Orange (#E67E22)  | Gratitude, day ending    |
| Isha     | Night       | Purple (#8E44AD)       | Peace, contemplation     |

### Luqmay Brand Integration
- **Keep:** Cream background (#F5F0E8), Gold accent (#F5B526), White cards, Lexend font
- **Add:** Prayer-specific colors, Islamic geometric patterns, subtle background textures
- **Result:** Brand-aligned + culturally authentic + child-friendly
